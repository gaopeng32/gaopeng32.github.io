Bio

I will be joining the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech as an Assistant Professor in Fall 2021. Previously, I was a postdoc in Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley (working with Dawn Song). I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Princeton University (working with Prateek Mittal and Sanjeev R. Kulkarni). I received my B.E. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I am broadly interested in security, privacy, and systems. My research focuses on creating secure and privacy-preserving systems to solve real-world challenges. My current interests include the domains of advanced threat protection for systems, threat intelligence, blockchain, IoT, and privacy-enhancing technologies. My research draws on techniques from machine learning, natural language processing, program analysis, and network science.

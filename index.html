Research

I am broadly interested in security, privacy, and systems. My current interests include the following domains:

  • System security: advanced threat protection through system auditing (USENIX ATC'18, USENIX Security'18, ICDE'21, VLDB'19 Demo, ICDE'20 Demo, ICDE'21 Demo), threat intelligence gathering and management (SIGMOD'21 Demo)
  • Network security: attacks and defenses in online social networks (ACM CCS'15, IEEE CNS'18, MIS2'18)
  • Blockchain: blockchain interoperability (ACM CCS'19a), blockchain security
  • Mobile security: detecting and characterizing mobile apps' undesired behaviors through icons (ACM CCS'19b, ICSE'19) and user comments (ICSE'21)
  • Privacy: data privacy compliance (POLY'19)

I enjoy building scalable, secure, and trustworthy systems to solve real-world challenges. My work has been adopted by companies like Microsoft, NEC Laboratories America, and Facebook.
